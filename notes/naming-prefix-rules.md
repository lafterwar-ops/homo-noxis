# Naming and prefix rules

## Core prefixes

- Sa- = Candidate
- Va- = Veil
- Ka- = Keeper

## Chapter 8

Before ceremony:

- Sa-Kailan
- Va-Raedin
- Ka-Syphiron

After ceremony:

- Va-Kailan
- Ka-Raedin
- Ka-Syphiron remains Ka-Syphiron until death/martyrdom reference.

## Rule

Prefixes are not decoration. They alter social reality.
