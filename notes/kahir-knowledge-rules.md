# Kahir knowledge rules

Knowledge is divided by Kahir.

- Light: optics, sightlines, lenses, refraction, diffraction, interference, hidden writing.
- Faith: music, rhythm, echo, resonance, prayer, shaped sound, stone response.
- Balance: water, resources, biology, chemistry, fermentation, transformation, pressure.
- Justice: records, law, probability, counting, ruin, sequence.
- Shield: containment, secrecy, perimeter, threat definition, emergency seals.

Cross-Kahir knowledge is dangerous. Admiration and analogy are allowed; possession is not.
